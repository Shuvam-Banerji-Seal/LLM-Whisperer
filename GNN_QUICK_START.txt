💡 PRO TIPS:

1. Start with GCN - it's the simplest and most foundational
2. Use PyTorch Geometric - best docs and community
3. Mini-batch training is essential for graphs > 100K nodes
4. Always use early stopping on validation loss
5. Residual connections needed for depth > 4 layers
6. Check production section for scalability insights
7. Follow learning paths suited to your role
8. Verify results against official benchmarks
