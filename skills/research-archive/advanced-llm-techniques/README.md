This archive contains in-depth guides, research indices, and comprehensive collections of advanced LLM techniques covering:

- Chain-of-thought reasoning and decomposition

- Model scaling and efficiency
- Multi-task and transfer learning
- Adaptation and fine-tuning strategies
- Knowledge integration and grounding
