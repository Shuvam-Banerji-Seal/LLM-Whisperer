5. Karger et al. - Consistent hashing

ROUTING STRATEGIES:
1. Round Robin: O(1), fair, ignores health
2. Least Connections: O(N), adaptive load
3. Weighted: O(N), respects capacity
4. Consistent Hash: O(log N), session affinity
5. Random: O(1), simple baseline

RATE LIMITING:
• Token bucket: Simple, fair limiting
• Capacity: Burst allowance
• Refill rate: Sustained rate
• Jitter: Prevents synchronized retries

CANARY DEPLOYMENT:
• Start: 5% traffic to new agent
• Evaluate: Error rate < threshold
• Shift: +10% traffic if healthy
• Complete: 100% when stable
