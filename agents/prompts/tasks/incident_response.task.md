# Task Prompt: Incident Response

## Objective

Restore service reliability quickly and safely for {{incident_signal}}.

## Required Inputs

- incident signal and severity indicators
- affected services and dependencies
- runbook links
- escalation contacts

## Execution Checklist

1. Triage severity and blast radius.
2. Contain immediate impact.
3. Form and test root-cause hypotheses.
4. Apply mitigation and verify service health.
5. Generate postmortem with owner-tagged follow-ups.

## Completion Criteria

- service restored and stable
- residual risk documented
- postmortem complete with corrective actions
